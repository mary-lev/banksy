That's controversial!' Justin Bieber gets a tattoo of a Banksy... and the street artist is not impressed

This news was published by a British daily news website DailyMail in 2014. The Canadian singer Justin Bieber had the girl from Girl With Balloon tattooed onto his arm in 2014 and posted a photo of it on his Instagram. Banksy quickly shared the picture on his Facebook page, commenting simply: “Controversial”. Also DailyMail published a photo of Justin Bieber’s tattoo.
