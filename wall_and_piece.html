Wall And Piece by Banksy

This is a cult book in which Bansky presents for the first time the essentials of his multifaceted work. The book was published in 2005. Banksy, Britain's now-legendary "guerilla" street artist, has painted the walls, streets, and bridges of towns and cities throughout the world. Banksy's identity remains unknown, but his work is unmistakable with prints selling for as much as $45,000.
